oyakodon - one portion

1 chicken thigh, sliced into bite sized pieces
1/2 onion sliced
1 egg

sauce: 
2 tsp dashi powder
1 tbsp soy sauce
1/4 cup mirin (or 1/4 cup sake, 2 tsp sugar)

steps: 
1) simmer chicken, onions, sauce in covered pan
2) beat egg with some soy sauce and maybe some dashi powder
3) once chicken is done, pour egg mixture over top, and cover for maybe 30 secs until done to liking
4) slide pan contents on top of bowl of rice.
