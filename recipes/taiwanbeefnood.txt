Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup
(3 qt instant pot)

Ingredients
2 lbs beef chuck
1in ginger, smashed
4 cloves garlic
2 scallions
1 onion
1 tomato
1 tbsp tomato paste
1 tbsp broad bean sauce
1 tbsp rock sugar
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup shaoxing wine
1 spice packet for spiced foods


Steps
1) blanch and rinse beef
2) saute oil, ginger, garlic, scallions, onions in instantpot, until onions are translucent
3) add tomatoes, meat
4) add tomato paste, broad bean sauce, sugar, soy sauce, shaoxing wine
5) pour ~2 cups water and spice packet (or until 2/3 pot full)
6) pressure cook 75 mins and immedietely release pressure afterwards (next time, try 60 mins, natural release)
