sacriligeous tiramisu (no raw egg)

Ingredients: 
1 cup heavy cream
8 oz 1 cup marscapone cheese
1/4 cup of sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp amaretto

~20 ladyfingers
1 cup coffee, room temp
1 tbsp rum

Steps: 
1) beat heavy cream, sugar, vanilla extract until soft peaks
2) add marscapone cheese and amaretto, beat until stiff peaks
3) mix coffee and rum in small dish
4) quickly dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture before placing in first layer of serving dish 
5) spread some of cream mixture over ladyfingers as second layer
6) repeat 4/5 until all done
7) cocoa powder on top!
