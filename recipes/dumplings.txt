Dumplings

Ingredients: 
1 package dumpling wrappers (about 45?)
3/4 lb veggie (chives, napa cabbage, etc)
3/4 lb meat (fatty ground pork, shrimp, etc)

1/4 cup shaoxing wine
2-3 tbsp sesame oil
1 tsp salt
1 tbsp soy sauce
1/8 tsp white pepper


Steps: 
1) mix the finely chopped veggies and meat together in bowl
2) add 1/4 cup shoaxing wine, 2 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1/8 tsp white pepper, into bowl
3) mix with chopsticks (for like 5 mins) until very soft and cohesive
4) wrap the dumplings
5) cook the dumplings
