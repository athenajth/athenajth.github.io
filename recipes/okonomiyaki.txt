Okonomiyaki
The version without the slimy yam (Yamaimo/Nagaimo)

Ingredients: 
  ~500g cabbage
  80g all purpose flour
  115g (1/2 cup) dashi
  2 eggs
  1/2 tsp salt
  1/4 tsp baking powder 

  More optional ingredients: 
  Bacon
  Green onions
  Pickled red ginger
  Tenkasu

Toppings: 
  Kewpie Mayo
  Okonomi Sauce

  More optional toppings
  Aonori
  Bonito flakes

Steps: 
  1) Mix together all ingredients other than cabbage together
  2) Finely chop up and rinse cabbage, less than 1 cm squared
  3) Mix cabbage into batter
  4) Preheat crepe pan, medium-low heat
  5) Bacon on the pan, cook until some fat starts rendering
  6) Scoop up heaping 1 cup worth of batter, pour into pan on top of bacon, and flatten until pancake shape ~1 cm thick
  7) Cook each side about 4 mins, but really depends on how thick it is
  8) Plate by spreading okonomi sauce and kewpie mayo on top, along with any toppings you want.
