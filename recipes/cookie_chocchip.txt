Chocolate Chip Cookies 
(~2 dozen)

Ingredients:
2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter
340g bread flour
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
56 g granulated sugar
226g light brown sugar
1 large egg
1 large egg yolk
2 tablespoons whole milk
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
350g chopped chocolate chips

Steps: 
1) melt 2 sticks of butter, then set aside in stand mixer bowl to cool slightly
2) whisk together the whole egg, egg yolk, milk, and vanilla extract in bowl 2
3) sift together flour, salt, baking soda in bowl 3
4) add sugars to butter in the stand mixer bowl, and beat with paddle on med speed for 2 mins
5) slow mixer to 'stir'; slowly mix in egg mixture (bowl 2) into stand mixer bowl, mix till throughly combined (30 secs)
6) slowly add dry ingredients (bowl 3), until halfway combined with streaks of flour still remaining
7) add chocolate, and mix just till combined
8) chill dough from anywhere from 1 hour to overnight

9) heat oven to 350F
10) scoop cookie dough onto baking sheets, bake for ~15 mins, rotating pan halway through
11) remove cookies from baking sheet, let cool for at least 5 mins before eating
