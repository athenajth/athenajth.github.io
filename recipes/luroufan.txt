Lu Rou Fan (Taiwanese Braised Pork)

Based off https://thewoksoflife.com/lu-rou-fan-taiwanese-braised-pork-rice-bowl/, but with a lot of influence from family recipe. 

Ingredients: 
1 lb pork belly
1 lb ground pork
1 onion, diced

1/2 oz rock sugar (or 3 tsp granulated sugar)
1/4 cup shaoxing wine
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 cups water

10 shiitake mushrooms
dried shallots
pickled lettuce
5 spice tofu

spice packet

Steps: 
1) blanch pork belly. cut into 1/2 inch pieces and sear. 
2) brown ground pork
3) cook sugar until melted, then add onion,
