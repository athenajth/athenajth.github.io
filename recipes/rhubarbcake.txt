Rhubarb Cake
made in 10in cast iron

Ingredients:
120g (1 cup) all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
2 large eggs
1 large egg yolk
300g (1.5 cups) sugar
4 tbsp melted unsalted butter, cooled
1/4 cup greek yogurt (or sour cream)
2 tbsp dark rum
2 rhubarb stalks

Steps: 
0) preheat oven to 350F, butter/flour/prepare pan
0) melt 4 tbsp butter
1) whisk 1 cup APF, baking powder, salt in bowl 1
2) whisk eggs, egg yolk, 1.5 cups sugar until pale and thick (~1 min) in bowl 2
3) whisk melted butter, greek yogurt, rum in bowl 3
4) whisk butter mixture (bowl 3) into egg mixture (bowl 2) just till combined
5) add dry ingredients (bowl 1) into other bowl (now bowl 2) and fold in until batter is smooth
6) scrape into pan, chill 10 mins to let batter set

7) arrange and rest rhubarb in pretty pattern on top of batter
8) sprinkle with sugar
9) bake until golden on top, browned around the sides (45-55 mins)
